Incubation Model

A repeatable framework that moves from Problem -> Prototype -> Minimum Awesome Product in about 2 months.

01

Problem

Define the business problem, target users, and measurable outcomes before building.

02

Prototype

Build a testable solution slice to validate feasibility, user value, and workflow fit.

03

Minimum Awesome Product

Convert the prototype into a launch-ready MAP with integrations and automation in about 2 months total.

Case Studies

Every engagement is documented as Problem -> Prototype -> Outcome. Sensitive implementation details are redacted where required.

Internal

Business Card Intelligence and Follow-up Automation

Problem: Internal GTM teams captured business cards at events but manually entered contacts, causing slow follow-up and missed opportunities.

Prototype: Built a scanner tool that extracts card data, enriches contact profiles with LinkedIn information, and syncs records automatically.

Outcome: Teams can trigger immediate email outreach and automated follow-up sequences without manual chasing.

Internal CRM and campaign workflow details are intentionally redacted.
